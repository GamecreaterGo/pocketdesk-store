We got tired of choosing between power and portability. So we stopped choosing.

How It Started

One too many cramped coffee shop sessions.

PocketDesk started with a simple frustration. Our founder was writing on a phone on a train, wishing for a real keyboard — and realizing every "pocket keyboard" on the market felt like a compromise. Tiny keys. Flimsy plastic. Dead batteries at the worst moment.

So we spent 18 months obsessively prototyping. 47 physical iterations. Real-world testing in coffee shops, airports, trains, parks, and co-working spaces across 12 cities. The Foldable Bluetooth Pocket Keyboard was the result: a product that doesn't ask you to compromise.

That same obsession drives every product we release. We make fewer things. But everything we make has earned its place.

What We Believe
